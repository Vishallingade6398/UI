Solapur District is a district in Maharashtra state of India. The city of Solapur is the district headquarters. It is located on the south east edge of the state and lies entirely in the Bhima and Seena basins. The entire district is drained by the Bhima River.olapur District was ruled by various dynasties such as Andhrabhratyas, Chalukayas, Rashtrakutas, Yadavas, Bahamanis and many others from the historical period to pre British time. It is the fourth large district in Maharashtra in terms of land area and seventh large in terms of population.
